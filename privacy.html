Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Vyre. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Discord bot.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Discord User Data: User ID, username, and server information necessary for bot functionality
  • Message Data: Messages sent to the bot for processing AI responses and maintaining conversation context
  • Usage Data: Command usage statistics and interaction patterns to improve service quality

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide AI-powered responses and assistance
  • Maintain conversation context and memory features
  • Improve bot functionality and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All information is encrypted in transit and at rest. We store data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

Third-Party Services

Vyre uses Google's Gemini AI for natural language processing. Your messages may be processed by Gemini's API in accordance with Google's API Terms.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or using the data management features in your dashboard. We will delete your information within 30 days of your request.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection features

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the bot or our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, please contact us through our Dashboard or via Discord.
